Why Ghulduba

  • Ghulduba
  • Ancestry

Family is the root of all human beings. Over the years, I have met with many from different generations of our family. One of the biggest challenges has been to put all the pieces together. Our elder generation gave us a great foundation to live good lives, and it is only right we do what we can to preserve their identity and maintain family ties. I have tried various methods, even A1 paper, but have lost count of the number of attempts. I felt it’s time to go digital, thus ghulduba.com was started.

We hope this is a means of tracing back ones roots and knowing how our family is linked together.
